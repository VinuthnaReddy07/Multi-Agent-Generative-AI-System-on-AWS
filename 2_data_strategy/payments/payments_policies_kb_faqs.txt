**Payments Support KB and FAQs for Cafe (Bagels, Donuts, Drinks)**

1. **Can I pay with cash at the store?**
   Yes, we accept cash payments at all store locations. Simply let the cashier know your preference at checkout.

2. **What digital payments do you support?**
   We accept all major credit and debit cards, as well as Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other contactless payment methods.

3. **Can I pay online and pick up in store?**
   Yes, you can place your order and pay online, then choose the pickup option during checkout for a seamless experience.

4. **Do you offer refunds for incorrect orders?**
   Yes, if there's an issue with your order, please contact us within 24 hours. Verified issues will be refunded to your original payment method.

5. **Can I split payment between two methods?**
   Yes, in-store you can split payments between two cards or a combination of cash and card. Let the cashier know beforehand.

6. **Is tipping supported on card payments?**
   Yes, you’ll be prompted to add a tip when completing your card payment. Tipping is optional but appreciated.

7. **Can I use gift cards to pay?**
   Yes, we accept both physical and digital versions of our store’s gift cards. Enter the code at checkout or present it in-store.

8. **Why was my card declined?**
   Your card might be declined due to insufficient funds, incorrect billing info, or fraud prevention. Try another payment method or contact your bank.

9. **Do you store my payment information?**
   No, we never store your card details. All transactions are securely processed by our payment provider.

10. **Can I get a receipt by email or SMS?**
    Yes, digital receipts are available. You can opt to receive one via email or SMS at the time of purchase.

11. **Can I use rewards points to pay for my order?**
    Yes, if you’re enrolled in our loyalty program, you can redeem points for discounts at checkout.

12. **What happens if I get charged twice?**
    If you notice a duplicate charge, contact us immediately. Once verified, we’ll issue a refund for the extra charge.

13. **Is there a minimum purchase amount for card payments?**
    No, there is no minimum spend required when paying by card.

14. **Do you charge any transaction or service fees?**
    No, we do not add extra fees on top of the order total. What you see is what you pay.

15. **How do I update my saved payment method in the app?**
    Go to the app’s settings, navigate to Payment Methods, and update or delete your saved cards securely.

16. **Can I cancel an order and get my money back?**
    Yes, cancellations are allowed within 10 minutes of placing the order. Refunds will be processed to your original payment method.

17. **Are pre-paid cards accepted?**
    Yes, most pre-paid Visa, MasterCard, or Amex cards are accepted like regular credit/debit cards.

18. **Do you accept mobile wallets like Samsung Pay?**
    Yes, we accept Samsung Pay and similar mobile wallets that support NFC transactions.

19. **What if my gift card doesn't work?**
    Ensure the balance is valid and the code entered correctly. If problems persist, contact our support team for assistance.

20. **Is payment required when placing a catering order?**
    Yes, catering orders must be prepaid in full at the time of booking to confirm the order.
