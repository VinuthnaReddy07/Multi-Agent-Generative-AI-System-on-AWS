PROMOTION-RELATED KNOWLEDGE BASE FOR OFFERS EXPERT

1. Promo Eligibility Rules:
Defines who qualifies for a promotion. Some promos are exclusive to new users, members of certain loyalty tiers, or customers in specific regions. Eligibility is typically determined at checkout based on account metadata.

2. Promo Stacking Policy:
Clarifies whether multiple promotions can be combined. For example, a user may not be able to use a discount code and redeem loyalty points in the same transaction. Stacking rules are displayed during offer application.

3. Promo Code Troubleshooting:
Covers common reasons why a code might not work: expired, invalid format, usage cap reached, case sensitivity, or ineligibility. Suggests checking terms or contacting support.

4. Redemption Channels:
Lists where a promotion can be applied: in-app, in-store, drive-thru, or online. Some promos are channel-exclusive. Redemption method is shown in offer details.

5. Loyalty Point Use Rules:
Outlines minimum spend thresholds, restrictions on what items points can be used for, and how to apply them. Some items (e.g., gift cards) may be excluded.

6. Refunds and Promotions:
Explains how refunds impact promotional value. If a promo is used and the order is canceled or refunded, the promo might not be reusable unless explicitly stated.

7. Promo Types:
Describes types of offers—percent off, dollar off, free item with purchase, BOGO (buy one get one), or loyalty multiplier (e.g., 2x points).

8. Promo Duration and Renewals:
Details when the offer starts and ends. Also notes if the promo is recurring (e.g., every Monday) or one-time. Automatic renewals may be included for loyalty milestones.

9. Targeted Promotions:
Explains that some offers are personalized based on user behavior, purchase history, or region. These may not be visible to all users.

10. Out-of-Stock Handling:
Clarifies what happens if a promo item is unavailable. Customers may receive a substitute, delay, or cancellation message. App notifies user before checkout.

11. Time-Limited Flash Offers:
Promos valid for only a few hours or a single day. Users must act quickly. Push notifications or banners usually alert users.

12. Usage Limits Per Customer:
Defines how many times a single customer can use a promo. For example, one-time use only, once per week, or a daily limit.

13. Geo-Fenced Promotions:
Promos that are only valid in certain zip codes or store locations. Often used to promote new stores or local events.

14. Promo for Group Orders:
Explains whether offers apply to group orders and if each member can apply their own code.

15. Age-Restricted Promotions:
Some discounts (e.g., senior or student discounts) require age verification. Must present valid ID or verify through app.

16. Exclusive Channel Offers:
Some promos are exclusive to iOS or Android users, or only through specific marketing emails or campaigns.

17. Social Media Campaigns:
Covers promo codes released via Instagram, Twitter, or influencer partnerships. These often have usage caps or short lifespans.

18. Holiday and Seasonal Offers:
Outlines recurring offers for holidays (e.g., Valentine’s, Halloween). These may follow consistent patterns year-over-year.

19. Feedback-Based Rewards:
Some promos are given in exchange for completing surveys or providing feedback. Details how and when these are granted.

20. Referral Promotions:
Describes how users earn credit or promos by referring friends. Lists referral link mechanics, rewards, and restrictions.

1. **What types of promotions do you offer?**
   We offer seasonal discounts, BOGO deals, loyalty rewards, and limited-time promo codes.

2. **How can I find current offers?**
   Check our mobile app, website, or in-store displays for ongoing promotions.

3. **Can I use multiple offers at once?**
   Unless explicitly stated, promotions cannot be combined or stacked.

4. **Why didn't my promo code work?**
   It may be expired, case-sensitive, or not applicable to your cart items.

5. **Is there a student or senior discount?**
   Yes, students and seniors receive 10% off in-store with valid ID.

6. **Do I need to sign in to use a promotion?**
   Yes, most offers are tied to your loyalty account and require sign-in.

7. **Are offers valid at all locations?**
   Some offers are location-specific. Check the terms for store eligibility.

8. **Can I redeem promotions in the drive-thru?**
   Yes, most promos are valid via drive-thru unless noted otherwise.

9. **Do offers apply to delivery orders?**
   Some promos apply to delivery; check the app for eligible options.

10. **Can I apply a discount after the order is placed?**
    No, discounts must be applied before finalizing payment.

11. **Why was my discount removed at checkout?**
    It may not meet the required cart value or include excluded items.

12. **Are there bundle discounts available?**
    Yes, we periodically offer bundle pricing on drinks + donuts or bagels.

13. **How often do new promotions launch?**
    We introduce new offers monthly, and during holidays or special events.

14. **Do loyalty points affect promotional pricing?**
    No, promotional discounts are separate from loyalty redemptions.

15. **Can I use a promo code with gift card payment?**
    Yes, promo codes can be applied regardless of payment method.

16. **I forgot to apply my code. Can it be refunded?**
    Unfortunately, we can't retroactively apply promotions after purchase.

17. **Are there app-exclusive offers?**
    Yes, app users may receive special promotions and early access deals.

18. **Can I sign up for email promotions?**
    Yes, subscribe via our website or app settings to receive promo emails.

19. **What happens if a promo item is out of stock?**
    We may substitute with an equivalent product or apply the discount to a similar item.

20. **How do I report a promotion issue?**
    Contact our support via app or website with your order details and promo code used.
