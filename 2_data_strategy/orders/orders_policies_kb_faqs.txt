CANCELLATION POLICY:
You can cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it through the app or website. If your order hasn’t entered preparation yet, it will be cancelled without charge. If the order has already started being prepared, cancellation may not be possible. In such cases, support may review and decide if a refund is appropriate. Duplicate orders or technical issues may qualify for cancellation exceptions.

WRONG ITEM RECEIVED:
If you received the wrong item, let us know what you ordered and what you got. We’ll verify and offer one of the following: a refund for the incorrect item, a credit for your next purchase, or redelivery if available. Repeated issues from the same store will be reviewed for quality.

MISSING ITEM IN ORDER:
If something is missing from your order, please report it promptly. Once confirmed, we’ll refund the item or provide a store credit. We track such issues and take them seriously.

COLD OR POOR-QUALITY ITEMS:
If your drink or food arrives cold, stale, or not as expected, reach out to us. We may offer a refund, store credit, or coupon for a future order. Photos can help us resolve the issue faster.

CUSTOMIZATION NOT HONORED:
If your customization (like oat milk or no sugar) wasn’t followed, tell us what you ordered. Once verified, you’ll get a refund or credit. If it involved an allergy concern, we’ll escalate it to our health and safety team.

ORDER DELAYED:
If your order is delayed more than 10 minutes from the expected time, we’ll inform you of any known issues. For delays over 15 minutes, we may offer a coupon or partial refund depending on your experience.

ORDER NOT RECEIVED:
If your order is marked as delivered but you didn’t get it, report the issue. We’ll investigate with our delivery partner. If the order is confirmed lost, we’ll issue a refund or send a replacement.

PAYMENT ISSUES:
If you were charged twice or your payment failed, contact us with the transaction info. Most billing issues are resolved in 3–5 business days. You’ll get a reference number to track the resolution.

REORDERING:
You can reorder your last drink or meal through your order history. If an item is unavailable, we’ll let you know and suggest alternatives.

FEEDBACK:
We appreciate your feedback. Whether it’s about food quality, speed, or service, we listen. Sometimes, we may thank you with a coupon for your time and insights.

FRAUD PREVENTION:
If we notice suspicious activity like repeated refund claims, we may contact you for verification. Our goal is to ensure your account is safe and protected from misuse.

ESCALATION:
If you’re not satisfied with the resolution provided, you can request to escalate your concern. A senior team member will review the full conversation and help reach a resolution.

ALLERGY CONCERNS:
If you have a food allergy or sensitivity, always specify your preferences clearly at checkout. If your item was not prepared according to your allergen request, stop consumption and report it immediately. We take allergen mishandling seriously and will investigate the situation and issue a full refund or replacement.

STORE EXPERIENCE ISSUES:
If you had an issue at a physical location (rude behavior, cleanliness, long wait), please share store location and time of visit. We will route your feedback to the local manager and follow up if needed.

ACCOUNT OR APP PROBLEMS:
If you’re having trouble logging in, updating your profile, or experiencing app glitches, let us know. You can also try reinstalling the app or checking for updates. We’ll help restore access or report bugs to our tech team.

GIFT CARD ISSUES:
If your gift card didn’t apply correctly or the balance appears incorrect, send us the card number or transaction ID. We’ll review and adjust the balance if needed.

PROMO CODE NOT WORKING:
If a promo code fails, check the terms (expiry, item eligibility, minimum spend). If the code is still valid and not working, contact us and we’ll apply the discount manually or issue store credit.

ORDER MODIFICATIONS:
If you want to make changes to an order after placing it—like changing the drink size or switching a donut—contact us immediately. If the order hasn’t started preparation, we’ll try our best to accommodate the request.

RECEIPT OR INVOICE REQUESTS:
Need a receipt or invoice for your order? You can request a copy through the app or by contacting us with your order ID. We’ll email it to you or make it available in your order history.

UNAVAILABLE OR OUT-OF-STOCK ITEMS:
If an item becomes unavailable after your order is placed, we’ll inform you as soon as possible. You may choose a replacement or receive a refund for that item.

ORDER HISTORY ACCESS:
You can view your past orders in your account’s order history section. This allows you to track your previous meals, reorder favorites, or download receipts.
