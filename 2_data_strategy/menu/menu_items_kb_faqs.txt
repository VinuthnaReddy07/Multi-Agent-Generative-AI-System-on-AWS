Frequently Asked Questions:

1. What’s the difference between espresso and drip coffee?
Espresso is a concentrated shot brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee under pressure, while drip coffee uses gravity to slowly pass water through coarser grounds.

2. Is cold brew stronger than regular iced coffee?
Yes. Cold brew typically has more caffeine and a smoother, less acidic taste because it’s steeped for hours in cold water.

3. What’s the best milk alternative for a creamy texture?
Oat milk is the most popular dairy-free option for creaminess, followed by soy milk.

4. Can I order a drink with no added sugar?
Yes. Many drinks can be made sugar-free by omitting syrups or using sugar-free alternatives like stevia or sugar-free vanilla.

5. How much caffeine is in a typical drink?
It varies: espresso (~90 mg), cold brew (~200 mg), latte (~150 mg), chai (~60 mg), green tea (~30 mg).

6. Are all drinks available iced?
Most coffee and tea drinks can be served iced upon request, except some specialty lattes and hot-only seasonal options.

7. What are the most popular donut and drink pairings?
Glazed donut with latte, chocolate donut with cold brew, and maple donut with americano are top picks.

8. Can I customize the sweetness level of my drink?
Yes. You can request half-sweet, sugar-free, or add extra syrup as needed.

9. Are there any gluten-free donut options?
Currently, all donuts contain gluten. Gluten-free options are in consideration for future menus.

10. What is matcha and how is it different from green tea?
Matcha is powdered green tea whisked into milk or water, offering a stronger flavor and more antioxidants than traditional steeped green tea.

Latte: A Latte is a classic espresso-based drink made with one shot of espresso and steamed milk, topped with a thin layer of foam. It’s typically served hot in an 8 to 12 oz cup. Available sizes: small, medium, large. Flavor profile: smooth, creamy, mildly sweet. Common customizations include adding syrups (vanilla, caramel), switching to oat or almond milk, and extra shots. Sweetener options include stevia, honey, and sugar-free syrups. Best paired with a glazed donut. Contains about 150 mg of caffeine and 130–220 calories. Preparation: hand-crafted by barista. Allergy info: contains dairy; cross-contamination with gluten possible. Not sugar-free by default.

Cold Brew: Cold Brew is made by steeping coarse coffee grounds in cold water for 12–24 hours and served chilled. Available sizes: medium, large. Flavor profile: smooth, bold, low-acidity. Common customizations: splash of milk, sweet cream, or flavored syrup. Sweetener options: sugar-free syrup, honey. Best paired with chocolate donut. Caffeine: ~200 mg. Calories: 0–5 when served black. Preparation: slow cold steep. Allergy info: naturally dairy-free, sugar-free, gluten-free.

Americano: An Americano is made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water. Served hot or iced. Sizes: small, medium. Flavor profile: bold, clean, slightly bitter. Often customized with a splash of milk or a sweetener. Sweetener options: stevia, classic syrup. Best with maple donut. Caffeine: ~120 mg. Calories: near zero. Preparation: pulled espresso, hot water. Allergy info: free from dairy, sugar, gluten.

Mocha: A Mocha combines espresso, steamed milk, and chocolate syrup. Served hot or iced. Sizes: medium, large. Flavor: rich, chocolaty, sweet. Customizations: non-dairy milk, sugar-free chocolate, whipped cream. Sweetener options: chocolate syrup, sugar-free chocolate. Best paired with cinnamon sugar donut. Caffeine: ~130 mg. Calories: 250–350. Preparation: hand-mixed by barista. Allergy info: contains dairy and chocolate; gluten-free by ingredients.

Espresso: Espresso is a concentrated coffee shot made by forcing hot water through finely-ground beans. Served in 1–2 oz size. Flavor: strong, intense, slightly bitter. Customizations: extra shot, splash of milk. No sweetener by default. Best with Boston cream donut. Caffeine: ~90 mg. Calories: <5. Preparation: pulled shot. Allergy info: naturally dairy-free, sugar-free, gluten-free.

Chai Latte: A Chai Latte blends black tea, spices, and steamed milk. Served hot. Sizes: medium, large. Flavor: spiced, aromatic, creamy. Customizations: oat milk, less sweet. Sweetener: honey, sugar-free syrup. Best with chocolate donut. Caffeine: ~60 mg. Calories: 200–250. Preparation: brewed chai base. Allergy: contains dairy; customizable; gluten-free by default.

Green Tea: Green Tea is brewed from green tea leaves. Served hot or iced. Sizes: small, medium. Flavor: grassy, mild, earthy. Common customizations: lemon, honey. Sweetener options: honey, stevia. Best with glazed donut. Caffeine: ~30 mg. Calories: 0–5. Preparation: steeped. Allergy info: free from dairy, sugar, gluten.

Black Tea: Black Tea is steeped from black tea leaves. Served hot. Sizes: small, medium. Flavor: bold, tannic, slightly astringent. Customizations: milk, sweetener. Sweeteners: honey, sugar. Best with maple donut. Caffeine: ~40 mg. Calories: near zero unless sweetened. Allergy: free from dairy, sugar, gluten unless modified.

Matcha Latte: Matcha Latte is made with ground green tea powder and steamed milk. Served hot or iced. Sizes: medium, large. Flavor: earthy, slightly bitter, creamy. Customizations: dairy-free milk, extra matcha. Sweeteners: honey, vanilla. Best with cinnamon sugar donut. Caffeine: ~45 mg. Calories: 120–180. Prep: whisked and steamed. Allergy: contains dairy unless customized; gluten-free.

Glazed Donut: A yeast donut coated in sugar glaze. Size: single standard. Flavor: sweet, soft. Customizations: none. Best with coffee drinks. Sweeteners: sugar. Calories: ~250. Prep: baked then glazed. Allergy: contains dairy, gluten, sugar.

Chocolate Donut: A cake donut with chocolate glaze. Size: single. Flavor: rich, cocoa-heavy. No common customizations. Best with chai latte or espresso. Sweeteners: sugar. Calories: ~280. Prep: baked and topped. Allergy: contains dairy, gluten, sugar.

Maple Donut: A yeast donut with maple glaze. Size: single. Flavor: sweet, maple. Customizations: not common. Best with americano. Sweetener: maple glaze. Calories: ~270. Prep: baked and glazed. Allergy: contains gluten, dairy, sugar.

Boston Cream Donut: A filled donut with custard and chocolate glaze. Size: standard. Flavor: creamy, chocolaty. No customizations. Best with espresso. Sweeteners: sugar. Calories: ~300. Prep: filled and glazed. Allergy: contains dairy, gluten, eggs.

Cinnamon Sugar Donut: A donut coated with cinnamon sugar. Size: single. Flavor: spiced, sweet. Customizations: not typical. Best with green tea. Sweetener: sugar. Calories: ~260. Prep: rolled in sugar blend. Allergy: contains dairy, gluten, sugar.
